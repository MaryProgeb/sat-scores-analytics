# Analysis of SAT scores in US schools

SAT, or Scholastic Aptitude Test, is a test that high school seniors in the U.S. take every year. The SAT has three sections, each is worth 800 points. Colleges use the SAT to determine which students to admit. High average SAT scores are usually indicative of a good school.

The aim of the project is to explore the relationship between the SAT score and different factors.
